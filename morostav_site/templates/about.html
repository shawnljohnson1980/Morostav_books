About the Author

Moros’ Tav is a multi-talented individual...

Author Bio: Moros’ Tav is a multi-talented individual whose creative prowess spans a diverse range of disciplines, from photography and writing to mechanical and survival skills. A seasoned learner, Tav is driven by a relentless curiosity to understand and master everything from the technicalities of building to the art of storytelling.

Growing up with a strong work ethic and the influence of a mother who taught him the value of imagination and independent thinking, Tav embraced life’s challenges as learning opportunities. Drawing inspiration from his experiences in photography, he captures emotions through the lens, blending moments of beauty with layers of depth. His skills in

photography and graphic design complement his writing, where he explores themes of resilience, personal growth, and the power of human connection. Moros’ Tav’s passion for self-sufficiency and independence is a recurring theme in both his personal life and his creative work. A former soldier with a deep respect for family values and the importance of

preparation, he draws from a lifetime of knowledge to create stories that resonate with readers seeking authenticity and a sense of purpose. When he’s not penning his next novel, Tav can be found cultivating his homestead, teaching his children, and working to build a future where technology and self-reliance coexist. His latest work, Precarious, weaves together the threads of love, human connection, and the boundaries we break to truly understand one another.
